Rules

Your main goal is to guess the other player's card by asking questions about his card and acting upon it.

Follow the bottom right corner where your status is updated on every turn. It shows what you are supposed to do.

Each player goes through the same cycle of asking a question, waiting for an answer and flipping cards or guessing the right one.

When you have received an answer, you can click on the cards which will trigger a popup. After that, you can flip a card or guess.

Be careful when guessing, you only have a single chance!
